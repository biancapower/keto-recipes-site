Keto Broccoli Salad

Approx. 35 minutes to make.

Ingredients

Salad

  • kosher salt
  • 3 heads broccoli, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup toasted sliced almonds
  • 3 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped chives

Dressing

  • 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp. apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp. dijon mustard
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a medium pot or saucepan, bring 6 cups of salted water to a boil. While waiting for the water to boil, prepare a large bowl with ice water.
  2. Add broccoli florets to the boiling water and cook until tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and place in the prepared bowl of ice water. When cool, drain florets in a colander.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk to combine dressing ingredients. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Combine all salad ingredients in a large bowl and pour over dressing. Toss until ingredients are combined and fully coated in dressing. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Cauliflower Pizza

Approx. 45 minutes to make.

Ingredients

Cauliflower Dough

  • 700g Cauliflower
  • 75g almond meal
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 30g grated parmesan
  • 2 eggs

Toppings

  • 60ml tomato pasta sauce
  • 60ml pesto
  • 100g mozarella, thinly sliced
  • 250g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Baby basil, to serve

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 140°C (fan-forced). Line 2 baking trays with baking paper. For cauli-flour dough, roughly chop cauliflower and place in a food processor. Pulse until very finely chopped, then tip into the centre of a clean Chux cloth. Place over a bowl and squeeze to remove any excess liquid. Spread cauliflower over prepared trays and bake for 15 minutes to dry out. Remove and stand to cool slightly.
  2. Return cauliflower to food processor with almond meal, oil, parmesan, a pinch each of salt flakes and freshly ground black pepper, and eggs. Whiz until a smooth dough, scraping sides if neccesary.
  3. Increase oven to 180°C. Spread cauliflower mixture over 1 baking-paper-lined tray to form a circle and use a piece of plastic wrap to press mixture down until about 1cm thick. Roast for 25 minutes or until cooked through and golden, then remove from oven.
  4. Increase oven to 250°C. Spread tomato sauce and half the pesto over cauliflower pizza base, then top with mozzarella and roast for 5-10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbling.
  5. Meanwhile, toss tomatoes and remaining pesto in a bowl. Season with pepper. Remove pizza from oven and top with pesto tomato and basil. Scatter over extra parmesan to serve.

Cheeseburger Sushi

Approx. 30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 16oz ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2oz cheddar cheese
  • 10 slices bacon
  • 1/2 medium red onion, sliced
  • 2 medium dill pickles, sliced
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2tbsp low-carb ketchup
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened relish

Instructions

  1. Gather and prep all ingredients. Pre-heat oven to 375F.
  2. On a piece of plastic wrap, lay out the slices of bacon slightly on top of one another.
  3. Spread the ground beef on top of the bacon. Spread it out thin to ensure it fully cooks. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the cheddar cheese, sliced red onion, and sliced dill pickles on top of the ground beef closest to you. Make a clean line so it is easy to roll.
  5. Roll the bacon tightly, enclosing everything inside, using toothpicks to keep it rolled if needed.
  6. Place the roll on a cookie sheet and bake for 40 minutes or until fully cooked through. While the cheeseburger sushi is cooking, make the dipping sauce. In a bowl combine mayonnaise, ketchup, yellow mustard, and dill pickle relish. Stir this together until it is fully incorporated, and set aside until ready.
  7. When slightly cooled, cut the ‘sushi’ into rolls, one per serving. Serve with the sauce.
