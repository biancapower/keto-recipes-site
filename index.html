The Ketogenic, or "Keto" diet, is a diet comprised of low-carbohydrate foods. The diet focuses on gaining energy from proteins and fats rather than carbohydrates. Most keto diets cut back on the most easily digestible carbs, such as sugar, white bread, sugary drinks and pastries. This is a collection of some of our favourite keto recipes from across the internet. All of these have been tried and taste-approved to make sure we're only giving you the best keto suggestions!
