Keto Pancakes with Berries and Cream

Approx. 25 minutes to make.

Ingredients

Pancakes

  • 4 eggs
  • 200 g (225 ml) cottage cheese
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) ground psyllium husk powder
  • 50 g butter or coconut oil

Toppings

  • 50g fresh raspberries or fresh blueberries or fresh strawberries
  • 225 ml heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Add eggs, cottage cheese and psyllium husk to a medium size bowl and mix together. Let sit for 5-10 minutes to thicken up a bit.
  2. Heat up butter or oil in a non-stick skillet. Fry the pancakes on medium-low heat for 3â€“4 minutes on each side. Don't make them too big or they will be hard to flip.
  3. Add cream to a separate bowl and whip until soft peaks form.
  4. Serve the pancakes with the whipped cream and berries of your choice.
